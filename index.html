Roach Ecosystem

Roach is a decentralized organization platform based on Huobi ECO Chain, which runs as a smart contract on HECO. Roach has a set of trays that can implement DAO management: organization module, voting module, token module and financial module. In an ideal situation, the price of Roach is controlled by the community.

Token total supply: 1000 billion

100% of all fee tokens will be deposited in AMM DEX

There is no ICO and no team dividends.

Scenarios
Service tip

We should be grateful to those who provide us with services, but when we don’t have enough dollars to pay, Roach is a good consumption. Pay tips at low cost anytime, anywhere. We will not worry about tipping because of the different rates of tipping in different countries and regions.

gift

As a gift to your friends, friends can store Roach in their wallets and wait for the appreciation. Such gifts do not need to be shipped and are more environmentally friendly. They can also record on the blockchain forever what you have given to your friends. gift. Fee keeps friendship alive.

appreciation

With the use of Roach and the passage of time. The number of Roach will become less and less, and more and more people will use it. It is a very good financial management method to store Fee in the wallet and wait for its appreciation.
